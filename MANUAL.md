# 📖 SmartQ User Manual

Welcome to the **SmartQ** User Manual. This guide provides step-by-step instructions for both Students and Administrators to navigate and utilize the system effectively.

---

## 🎓 Student Instruction Manual

### 1. Getting Started
- **Registration:** Visit the landing page and click **"Get Started"**. Fill in your First Name, Last Name, College, Year Level, Email, and Password.
- **Login:** Use your registered email and password to access your student dashboard.

### 2. Booking a Queue
- Navigate to the **"Book Queue"** section from your sidebar.
- Browse the available schedules. Each schedule shows the date, time slot, and remaining capacity.
- Click **"Book Now"** on your preferred slot.
- Once booked, your queue number will be generated automatically.

### 3. Tracking Your Position
- On your **Dashboard**, you will see your **Active Booking** card.
- **Live Position:** This shows how many people are currently ahead of you.
- **Status:** 
  - `Pending`: Waiting for your turn.
  - `Called`: It is your turn! Proceed to the validation counter.
  - `Validated`: Your ID has been successfully processed.

### 4. Managing Your Profile
- Go to the **"Profile"** page.
- You can view your account details (Name, College, Year Level). These are read-only to maintain system integrity.
- You can **update your password** at any time by entering a new one and clicking "Save Changes".

### 5. Need Help?
- Use the **"Get in Touch"** form on the landing page to send a message to the admin team.
- You can also click on the **Email Icon** in the Team section to copy the developer's email address for technical support.

## 🛡️ Administrator Instruction Manual

### 1. Admin Dashboard
- The dashboard provides a high-level overview of the system's performance.
- **Stats Cards:** Monitor total students, active queues, and validation rates.
- **Visual Charts:** View the distribution of students by college and hourly queue trends.

### 2. Managing the Queue
- Navigate to **"Manage Queue"**.
- Here you can see the list of students currently in line for the day.
- **Calling Students:** Click the "Call" button to notify a student that it is their turn.
- **Validation:** Once the student arrives at the counter, verify their ID and update their status to "Validated".

### 3. Creating & Managing Schedules
- Go to the **"Schedules"** section.
- **Add Schedule:** Define the date, start time, end time, and maximum student capacity.
- **Monitor Capacity:** Keep an eye on how many students have booked each slot to avoid over-congestion.

### 4. Student Validation & Reports
- Access the **"Students"** list to see all registered users.
- Use the **"Reports"** page to generate documentation for event attendance and validation audits.
- **Exporting:** You can download reports in **PDF** or **CSV** format for official record-keeping.

### 5. Admin Profile
- As an administrator, you can update your **Full Name** and **Email Address** in the Profile section.
- Secure your account by regularly updating your **Password**.

## ❓ Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

**Q: Can I change my booking time?**
*A: Currently, you must cancel your existing booking (if allowed) or wait for it to expire before booking a new slot. Contact Admin if you made a mistake.*

**Q: My queue number disappeared!**
*A: This usually happens if your session expired or if you have been marked as "Validated". Check your "History" page to see past activities.*

**Q: Why can't I edit my College in my profile?**
*A: To ensure data accuracy for reporting, personal details are locked. If your info is incorrect, please contact the System Administrator.*
